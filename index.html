Gusage is a tool for photographers, designers and other visual artists which aim to find using the web for your images and photographs, enabling you to:

  • Know where you work is being used
  • Check if they are meeting the standards for authorship defined in the publication of the image
  • See the impact of it in internet

Ask Gusage to load your gallery from flickr and perform the search for each one. You'll be able to:

  • Take a quick look of every result
  • Expand the info to see the additional information Gusage is able to collect from the referer page
  • Visit the referer page and check with your own eyes how other people use your own work
  • Mark the referer page as favourite or as bad/unallowed use to later actions
